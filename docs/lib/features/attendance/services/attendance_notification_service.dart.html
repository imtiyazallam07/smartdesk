attendance_notification_service.dart

lib/features/attendance/services/attendance_notification_service.dart

Overview

Technical Explanation

This service manages the lifecycle of local notifications. It uses a Forward-Scheduling Algorithm that iterates through the next 30 days, cross-references HolidayService to skip holidays/Sundays, and schedules zonedSchedule alarms with deep-linking payloads.

Layman Explanation

This is a Smart Alarm Clock. Instead of just ringing every single day, it checks your calendar first. If it sees a holiday or a Sunday coming up, it stays quiet. Every time you open the app, it looks 30 days into the future and sets up helpful reminders for your class days.
