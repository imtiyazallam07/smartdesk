lib/features/attendance/screens/quick_attendance_screen.dart

Overview

Technical Explanation

Optimized for One-Tap Interaction. This screen is typically launched from a notification background action. It uses a high-contrast Layout with large tap targets. Selecting a binary option (Present/Absent) performs an immediate database write, while "Partial" triggers a navigation to the full editor.

Layman Explanation

This is the Quick Mark popup. When you tap on your 6 PM reminder notification, this screen appears with large buttons for "Present", "Absent", or "Holiday". It lets you mark your whole day with one tap without having to open the main calendar.
