attendance.dart

lib/features/attendance/models/attendance.dart

Overview

Technical Explanation

Defines the DailyAttendance and AttendanceStatus models. It includes complex Serialization Logic (toMap/fromMap) that handles JSON encoding for mapping slot-specific presence data, ensuring that snapshots of subject names are preserved even if the master subject is deleted.

Layman Explanation

This is the Building Block of your attendance records. It's a structured piece of data that stores the date, whether you were present, and any notes you took. It ensures that your records are saved exactly the same way every time.
