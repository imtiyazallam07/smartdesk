todo_notification_service.dart

lib/features/todo/services/todo_notification_service.dart

Overview

Technical Explanation

Coordinates Time-Sensitive Task Alarms. It schedules weekly repeating notifications for habits and multiple countdown reminders (7, 3, 1 day) for deadlines. It uses high-priority Android channels and exact alarm modes to ensure reliability.

Layman Explanation

This is your Task Reminder System. It translates your deadlines into helpful "pokes" on your phone. If you have a big project due next week, it will quietly remind you a few days early, then a day before, and finally on the due date itself.
