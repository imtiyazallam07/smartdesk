Overview

Technical Explanation

The Unified Task Management Hub. It uses a TabController to toggle between RecurringTask and OneTimeTask entities. It displays active tasks in distinct lists, providing reactive checkboxes for completion and toggle switches for enabling/disabling habits.

Layman Explanation

This is your Master To-Do List. It's split into two parts: "Repeated" for habits you do every day (like Exercise) and "Tasks" for one-time things (like "Buy Groceries"). You can easily check things off or turn off reminders for habits you're pausing.
